checklist_section:
  tag: Before You Go
  heading: Your Caucus Checklist
  sub: Three simple steps to make sure you're ready for caucus night.
  steps:
    - item: "Confirm you are registered to vote in Utah at your current address — check at <a href=\"https://vote.utah.gov\" target=\"_blank\">vote.utah.gov</a>. To participate in Caucus Night you must be registered to vote, but you do not need to be registered as a Democrat."
    - item: Use the Caucus Locator to find your caucus location and note the address.

        email reminders and notifications about the meeting.
    - item: Plan to arrive by 6:30 PM for check-in and socializing — the formal
        meeting begins at 7:00 PM

timeline_section:
  tag: Night-of Schedule
  heading: Caucus Night Timeline
  items:
    - time: 6:30 PM
      title: Check-In Opens
      desc: Confirm your voter registration at the check-in table and find your
        precinct number before the meeting begins.
    - time: 7:00 PM
      title: Meeting Called to Order
      desc: The temporary Chair or county representative officially opens the caucus
        meeting. At this point, no new attendees may be added to the voter list.
    - time: 7:05 PM
      title: Welcome from the County Chair
      desc: A brief message from the county chair on the purpose of the caucus system
        and goals for the party.
    - time: 7:10 PM
      title: Nominations & Candidate Speeches
      desc: Nominations are opened for each position. Candidates announce their
        candidacy, give a brief speech (1–2 minutes), and answer questions from
        attendees.
    - time: 7:30 PM
      title: Voting
      desc: Verified attendees receive a paper ballot and vote for their preferred
        candidates. Two volunteers independently count all ballots.
    - time: ~8:00 PM
      title: Results Announced & Adjournment
      desc: The Chair announces vote totals and winners publicly. Results are recorded
        in the meeting minutes. Meeting is officially adjourned.
