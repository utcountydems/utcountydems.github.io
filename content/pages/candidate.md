prepare_section:
  tag: Before March 17
  heading: Prepare Your Campaign
  sub: The most successful caucus candidates start early. Here's what to do before
    caucus night.
  steps:
    - title: Announce Your Candidacy
      desc: Let your neighbors know you're running. The more visibility you have before the
        night, the better.
    - title: Prepare a 2-Minute Speech
      desc: "At the caucus, each candidate delivers a brief speech. Cover three
        things: who you are, why you're running, and your commitment to the
        position and the party. Practice it aloud — two minutes goes fast."
    - title: Know Your Precinct
      desc: Look up your precinct on the Precinct Locator. Know who your current Chair
        is, whether there's a Facebook group, and what your neighbors care
        about. Local knowledge makes a compelling candidate.
