  - id: "chair"

    icon: "fa-solid fa-star"

    desc1: "The Precinct Chair is the <strong>leader of the Democratic Party within your neighborhood</strong>. This is the most visible and impactful role available at the precinct level, connecting your neighbors directly to the county and state party."
    desc2: "Chairs serve as the primary point of contact between the precinct and the county party. They work with the county party to organize local activities, represent the precinct in the Central Committee, and help build the grassroots foundation that campaigns and candidates depend on."
    desc3: "Any registered Utah voter may run for Precinct Chair — no prior party affiliation or experience is required. A commitment to the community and the Democratic Party's values is what matters most."
    responsibilities:
      - item: "Plans and runs the next Neighborhood Caucus Night in 2028"
      - item: "Attends County Central Committee meetings throughout the year"
      - item: "Coordinates party initiatives and events in the neighborhood"
      - item: "Serves as the primary contact between precinct and county party"

  - id: "vicechair"
    name: "Precinct Vice-Chair"
    term: "2-Year Term"
    icon: "fa-solid fa-user-shield"
    icon_style: "background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1a3d6e, #38b6ff);"
    desc1: "The Precinct Vice-Chair is the <strong>second-in-command</strong> for the precinct's Democratic Party organization. The Vice-Chair and Chair work as a team, sharing the work of running an active precinct."
    desc2: "The Vice-Chair steps in to lead whenever the Chair is unavailable, ensuring continuous leadership for the precinct. Many Vice-Chairs specialize in a particular area — outreach, social media, or event coordination — that complements the Chair's strengths."
    desc3: "This role is an excellent path for someone who wants to be involved and build experience in party leadership without carrying the full weight of the Chair position alone."
    responsibilities:
      - item: "Acts as Precinct Chair in the Chair's absence"
      - item: "Assists with all precinct leadership duties alongside the Chair"
      - item: "Attends County Central Committee meetings"
      - item: "Often specializes in outreach, communications, or event planning"
      - item: "Helps recruit new members and volunteers in the precinct"

  - id: "treasurer"
    name: "Precinct Secretary / Treasurer"
    term: "2-Year Term"
    icon: "fa-solid fa-receipt"
    icon_style: "background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1a3d6e, #38b6ff);"
    desc1: "The Secretary or Treasurer assists the Precinct Chair and Vice-chair to manage accurate records."
    desc2: "These officers may also assist that Chair and Vice-chair in their duties to mobilize voters and connect the county party to local communities."
    desc3: "This role is an excellent path for someone who wants to start being involved at the precinct level."
    responsibilities:
        - item: Assist in the next Presidential Local Caucus Night
        - item: Assist the Precinct Chair in initiatives and efforts in the precinct
        - item: Maintain precinct membership records and positions
        - item: Track and ensure all contact data is current

  - id: "state-delegate"
    name: "State Delegate"
    term: "Convention Cycle"
    icon: "fa-solid fa-landmark"
    icon_style: "background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1a6b3a, #2e9050);"
    desc1: "State Delegates represent their precinct at the <strong>Utah State Democratic Nominating Convention</strong>, where they vote to select candidates for statewide offices including Governor, U.S. Senate, U.S. House, and statewide ballot positions."
    desc2: "Becoming a State Delegate is one of the most direct ways a citizen can influence who appears on the ballot. Delegates hear from candidates, ask questions, and cast binding votes that shape the party's nominee choices."
    responsibilities:
      - item: "Attend the Utah State Democratic Nominating Convention (in late April 2026)"
      - item: "Review and vet candidates running for statewide offices"
      - item: "Attend party organizing conventions to elect state party leadership"
      - item: "Represent your precinct's values and priorities in the nominating process"

  - id: "county-delegate"
    name: "County Delegate"
    term: "Convention Cycle"
    icon: "fa-solid fa-building-columns"
    icon_style: "background: linear-gradient(135deg, #6b3a1a, #904b2e);"
    desc1: "County Delegates represent their precinct at the <strong>Utah County Democratic Nominating Convention</strong>, where they vote to select candidates for county offices including County Commission, County Sheriff, County Clerk, and other local positions."
    desc2: "County-level races have a direct impact on local services, governance, and quality of life. County Delegates play a critical role in ensuring that Democratic candidates for these offices reflect the values of their communities."

      - item: "Review and vet candidates running for county offices"
      - item: "Vote on candidates for your House District, County Commission, Sheriff, Clerk, and other offices"
      - item: "Represent your precinct's interests in county-level Democratic nominating process"
